Per-phase verifier 5/5 must-haves passed on all three phases; the milestone audit (`v1.5-MILESTONE-AUDIT.md`) cleared 6/6 cross-phase risks (Phase 28 ↔ Phase 30 file overlap on `lib/slots.ts` / `app/api/slots/route.ts` / `booking-shell.tsx`; reschedule flow under Phase 28's BookingRow shape change; embed widget composition with Phase 30's `lg:` breakpoint; audience rebrand boundary; carryover hygiene).
