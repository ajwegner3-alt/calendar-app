<objective>
Restructure the public booker from a 2-column nested-grid layout (outer `[1fr_320px]` in `booking-shell.tsx` + inner `lg:grid-cols-2` in `slot-picker.tsx`) into a single flat 3-column grid owned by `booking-shell.tsx`. Calendar, time slots, and the form/placeholder render as direct children of one grid context.

Purpose: Unblock BOOKER-01..04. The current 2-column layout only shows times AFTER a date is picked, and the form column reflows when revealed. The 3-column layout shows all three regions simultaneously at desktop, with the form column reserved at a fixed 320px width before AND after slot pick (zero layout shift). Mobile collapses to natural single-column DOM order. Embed widgets at iframe widths < 1024px stay single-column automatically via the `lg:` breakpoint.

Output: Modified `booking-shell.tsx` (now grid owner + slots fetch owner), deleted (or empty/gutted) `slot-picker.tsx`. Pushed to `main` for live testing per global preferences.
</objective>
